# Acceptable AI Use Policy (v1.0)
**Scope:** All employees, contractors, and third-party partners.  
**Purpose:** To ensure compliance with the EU AI Act and protect company Intellectual Property (IP).

---

## 1. Approved AI Tools
Employees may only use AI tools that have been vetted and approved by the Security Team.
* **Approved:** [Company Enterprise ChatGPT], [Internal DevAI].
* **Banned:** All public, non-enterprise AI chatbots (e.g., personal accounts).

## 2. Data Classification Rules
**NEVER** input the following into any AI tool:
* Client Personal Identifiable Information (PII) / IBANs.
* Proprietary source code or "Secret Sauce" algorithms.
* Strategic board meeting notes or unpublished financials.

## 3. The "Human-in-the-Loop" Requirement
Per **Article 14 of the EU AI Act**, no output from an AI can be sent to a client or used to make a financial decision without a **human review**. 
* Any AI-generated content must be labeled as "AI-Assisted" in internal documentation.

## 4. Prohibited Uses
* **Deception:** Generating deepfakes or misleading content.
* **Bias:** Using AI to filter job applicants based on protected characteristics.
* **Automation:** Setting up "Auto-pilots" that access sensitive databases without supervision.

---

## 5. Compliance & Literacy
All employees must complete the **AI Literacy Assessment** annually. Failure to comply with this policy may lead to revocation of tool access and disciplinary action.
